Overview

Purpose

To provide access to information regarding the Chamber of Commerce for Aberdeen, ID. The name of the site will be "Aberdeen Chamber of Commerce". This name represents a town in which the chamber operates. Aberdeen Chamber of Commerce acts to promote the economic goals and events of the town and surrounding communities.

Scenarios

• What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking? • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors? • What has been the population growth in the area?
